How to Use

Overview: Welcome! You'll notice there are five colored buttons and a grid. This grid is called a "piano roll," and each clickable box represents a note; when you press the play button, we will play from left to right all the notes you selected. There are multiple clefs as well. Additionally, each colored box represents a "layer"; in other words, each one is a different sound, and when you combine them all, you get something fun!

To start, go ahead and add some notes to your first song. The first sound is selected by default, but feel free to go ahead and change it up.
